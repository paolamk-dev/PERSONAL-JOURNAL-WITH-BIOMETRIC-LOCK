## 🎨 Brand Identity Elements

### Logo
The SmartAC logo features:
- **MTP initials** (Makamte Tatsinkou Paola) as central typographic element
- **WiFi/IoT symbol** integrated into design
- **Airflow arc motifs** representing cooling
- **Wordmark:** "SMARTAC"
- **Tagline:** "Intelligent Climate. Total Control."

### Color Palette
| Color | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|-------|-----|-----|-------|
| **Arctic Blue** | #0A84FF | 10, 132, 255 | Primary brand color |
| **Cool Teal** | #30D5C8 | 48, 213, 200 | Accent, IoT highlights |
| **Deep Navy** | #0D1B2A | 13, 27, 42 | Dark backgrounds, text |
| **Frost White** | #F4F9FF | 244, 249, 255 | Light backgrounds |
| **Slate Grey** | #6B7280 | 107, 114, 128 | Secondary text |

### Typography
- **Primary Font:** Helvetica family
- **Hierarchy:** Bold for headers, regular for body
- **Sizes:** 36pt display down to 9pt captions

## 📝 Usage Guidelines

### DO:
✅ Use approved logo files
✅ Maintain clear space around logo
✅ Use exact brand colors
✅ Follow typography hierarchy
✅ Ensure WCAG contrast compliance

### DON'T:
❌ Stretch or distort logo
❌ Change brand colors
❌ Use on busy backgrounds
❌ Add effects or shadows
❌ Rotate or tilt logo
